So I Made a NFT, can I be Rich Now?

Mon Aug 30 2021
  • crypto
  • art

Yes, I'm responsible for the existence of this (opens new window). It's a 3d model that I made in Blender 3D and threw a filter over it with gimp. Not much thought went through it.

And, yes, I don't think that some millionaire will buy my stuff for the lols, but being resistant to new, dumb stuff just because it's new and dumb is... dumb. I just wonder if we could use the same principle to operate distributed Netflixes or Spotifies. Or Steams.

The current model is scarcity, not the recurring fees most of these platforms crave. What is lacking in podcasts, movies and games is maintenance, the ability to keep producing, since the file is endlessly reproducible.

There are single and multiple selling of NFTs, could there be a recurring sell option? A token that has a time validity to it? I don't know. But I wonder what will happen if crypto successfully maps all forms of human trading.
