Learning to Draw in a World of Generative AI

Fri Sep 22 2023
  • A.I.
  • Rant
  • Art

In the world of art, there's a journey we all embark upon, whether we're born with an innate talent or simply have a burning desire to create. My journey began with a stark comparison: the left picture, my humble creation from my first ever in-person art class, and the right one, a masterpiece generated through stable diffusion using the free tier of a Google Colab notebook. The transformation is undeniable, yet there's a lingering sense of "wrongness" in most AI art that keeps me grounded in my pursuit of becoming a proficient artist.

# The Artistry of Imperfection:

Comparing my own creations to what AI can conjure up can be disheartening. The stark difference in quality might discourage many, but I've come to realize that even in the age of advanced AI, there's a place for human-made art. The mistakes in AI-generated art highlights the irreplaceable role of artists[1], even those who consider themselves mediocre. AI (at the present moment, and without a high proficiency in prompt-crafting) makes mistakes that looks like an average of samples. Average of eye and hand positions, average of light and shadows, etc. Artists possess a unique ability to identify and, more importantly, imbue art with soul and emotion, something that AI has yet to master entirely.

# My Journey: Coder First, Artist Second:

As I embark on this artistic adventure, I can't deny that I may not be the most dedicated student in the art class. Striving for mediocrity may seem like an unusual approach, but for me, it's a "coder first, artist second" mentality. This journey is a testament to my determination to bridge the gap between technology and creativity, between what AI can do and what only a human hand can achieve.

# Conclusion:

The path to becoming an artist is not always a straightforward one. It's marked by self-discovery, imperfections, and the courage to embrace one's unique artistic journey. As I continue to learn how to draw, I'm excited to see where this path will lead me. Will I become a masterful artist, or will I simply strive for mediocrity? Either way, I know that in this digital age, there's still a place for human creativity, and I'm determined to explore it fully. Join me on this artistic adventure, and let's see where our journey takes us.
